### Game Concept: Age Matters — Age Logic Word Problem

This is a verbal word-problem game about relative ages. Two characters, Riya and Priya, each offer a clue about their age at a past or future point in time, and the student must work backwards from the clues to determine each character's current age and type both numbers into input fields. It trains algebraic reasoning with time-shifted ages.

### Core Mechanics & Interface

* **Header Stats:** A small circular avatar with a "Q1" label sits on the left, a central timer reads "00:02", and on the right a "0/10" star score with a light blue star icon.
* **Clue Layout:** Two stacked character-plus-speech-bubble groups mirror each other. **Riya's clue (row 1):** a thick **vivid-purple oval speech bubble** with a tail pointing bottom-right, reading "I was 12 years old, 4 years ago.", with Riya's avatar (girl with black braid, orange sari) placed to the bubble's right and her **"Riya" name label** sitting directly under the avatar. **Priya's clue (row 2):** the positions flip — Priya's avatar (yellow top, side ponytail) with her "Priya" label sits on the **left**, and the purple-bordered speech bubble with the tail pointing bottom-left sits to her right, reading "In two years, I will be three times as old as Riya was four years ago."
* **Question Prompt:** Below the two clue rows, a line reads "How old are Riya and Priya now?"
* **Input Fields:** Two stacked rows, each with a left-aligned label and a right-aligned rectangular input box with a thin black border and a light-gray **"Type here"** placeholder — "Riya's age is [Type here]" and "Priya's age is [Type here]".
* **Progression:** The chunk reports 10 total rounds of this puzzle type. The intro reads, "Check the clues given by Riya and Priya to find their age!"

### Behaviors and Interactions

* **Riya Age Input (Type):** The student taps the Riya field to bring up a keyboard and enters a numeric value.
* **Priya Age Input (Type):** The student taps the Priya field and types in a numeric value.

### Victory / Completion

Correct numerical ages for both characters entered into their respective input fields resolves the round.

### Variant Progression

The three variants share the same two-speech-bubble + two-input-field structure; only the character pair and the exact clue wording changes:
